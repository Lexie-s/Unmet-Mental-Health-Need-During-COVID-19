# Unmet Mental Health Need During COVID-19
A data analysis of the CDC Household Pulse Survey (2020–2022)

## Research Question
Which demographic groups experienced the largest disparity between 
reported need for and access to mental health care during COVID-19?

## Key Findings
- National unmet need peaked at 12.4% in January 2021
- Transgender adults had the highest unmet need of any group at 39.4%
- Demographic group membership explains 96.1% of variation in 
  unmet need (R²=0.961)
